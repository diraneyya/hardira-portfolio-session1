A Little About Me

My Story with Coding

Some people have started to code young they don't even remember when it first occured to them; others might have had more of an aha-moment when they discovered their love for code. For me, that realization came to me when I somehow ended up developing my own PC game Joyfess (now how that happened is a story for another day). I fell in love with coding! At the time, I was a project manager at a reputable multi-national organization and very content with my job and the amazing work culture. But eventually I did quit my job to enroll in masters of business analytics and information systems in the University of South Florida. For many that was for the sake of data science; for me, it was a stepping stone to shift into software engineering (it worked pretty well with the big overlap between the two worlds).

Today I'm a junior fornt end developer on a journey to learn more and more about HTML, JavaScript, CSS and React.
